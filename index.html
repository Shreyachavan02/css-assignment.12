Welcome to Pune - The Oxford of the East

Pune, Maharashtras cultural and educational capital, blends rich heritage with modernity.

About Pune

Pune is a historic and vibrant city known for its pleasant weather, top educational institutions, IT hubs, and cultural significance. It is home to historical landmarks such as Shaniwar Wada, Sinhagad Fort, and Aga Khan Palace.

Educational Hub

Pune is often called the Oxford of the East due to its prestigious institutions like Fergusson College, Pune University, and Symbiosis.

IT and Business Hub

Pune is a rapidly growing IT and business center, with major companies located in Hinjewadi, Magarpatta, and Kharadi. It has become a prime location for software engineers and entrepreneurs.

Tourist Attractions

From scenic forts to vibrant street life, Pune offers a mix of history and adventure. Popular tourist spots include Sinhagad Fort, Dagdusheth Ganpati, and Parvati Hill.
